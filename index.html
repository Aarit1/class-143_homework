AI PING-PONG




Instructions

  1. There are 2 paddles, the orange paddle is operated by the computer, and the red paddle is operated as per the movement of your right wrist.
  2. The goal of the game is to move the paddle so you will be able to hit the ball back until and you win when your opponent is unable to hit the ball back.
  3. To play, move your right wrist in an ascending and decending mannera and a red dot should appear on your right wrist. Now press the play button, and move yourself 3-4 feet away from your device and start PLAYING!
